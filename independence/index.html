Independence between Types and Terms in Simply Typed Lambda Calculus

  • stlc contains strengthening lemmas that show types in simply typed lambda calculus (STLC) do not depend on terms a) in an empty context , and b) in a context containing terms .

Additional Examples of Independence


Additional examples showing the differences between independence and subordination.
  • str. Given (p => q) => r => r, p is subordinate to r. However, r is independent of p.
  • str1. By adding a clause q => r that introduces the dependence between q and r, p is still subordinate to r. However, r is no longer independent of p.
